About Naturglimt


Winter season ☃️

Whether it be Christmas, Hanukkah, New Year's Eve or Valentine's day,
we make sure you have the perfect surroundings, menu and activities
to enjoy the very best this cold and festive season has to offer.

If you're lucky you might catch a glimpse of the stunning northern lights - ✧ *:･ﾟ✧



Activities

  • Skiing and snowboarding in Hafjell Ski Resort
  • Ice skating
  • Sleigh Rides and Dog Sledding
  • Northern Lights viewing
  • Winter spa & sauna
  • Christmas Markets

Slow-cooked pork neck with caramelized onions and mashed potatoes

Mocktail: Pomegranate juice with a splash of lemon and sparkling water
Hot Drinks: Hot cocoa with chili, or peppermint tea with honey
