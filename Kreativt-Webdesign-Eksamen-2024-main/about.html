About us

Welcome to Naturglimt Hotel
- Your seasonal escape in Hafjell, Norway

Founded in 2024 by 5 ambitious students at Kristiania who wanted to showcase the beauty
of seasons in Norway.

At Naturglimt, we believe that every season has a story to show and tell.
Nestled in the idyllic hills of Hafjell, our hotel offers a unique getaway
where the magic of nature meets Scandinavian comfort.

From the vibrant blooms of spring to the golden hues of autumn,
and from the long summer days to the senere snowy winters,
Naturglimt hotel is designed to celebrate the beauty of each season.
Our seasonal experiences ensure that every visit feels fresh,
exciting, and perfectly aligned with nature's rhythm.

From beautiful spring and summer weddings to cozy fall celebrations and Christmas,
we make sure you get the best of every event or holiday you wish to spend with us.



Why choose Naturglimt hotel?

  • Seasonal delights: Immerse yourself in our thoughtfully curated seasonal menus, activities and décor, tailored to reflect the charm of spring, summer, autumn and winter.
  • Comfort and Elegance: Enjoy our cozy, modern accommodations, offering panoramic views of Hafjell’s breathtaking landscape.
  • Perfect Location: Located at the heart of Norway’s natural splendor, we are just steps away from hiking trails, ski slopes, and local attractions.


Our mission

We aim to provide guests with a harmonious balance between nature and luxury. At Naturglimt Hotel, we are committed to creating lasting memories through exceptional service, sustainable practices, and a deep connection to the beauty of every magical season.

Whether you're seeking a tranquil retreat alone, an adventurous holiday with your family, or a romantic getaway, Naturglimt Hotel is your gateway to experiencing Hafjell's year-round beauty.






Our staff

Our goal is to make sure our guests feel at home when they visit us, so we have made sure to hire people who love the norwegian nature and hospitality as much as we do.

We have a big team of chefs, concierges, night auditors and maids who are more than willing to help if you need anything!
